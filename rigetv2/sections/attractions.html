Our Attractions
Our Giraffes: Euan and Evan

Giraffes

Euan and Evan are our tallest animals at the zoo. These gentle giants love to graze on the tallest trees in their habitat and can often be seen reaching for food with their long necks. A favorite for visitors to watch!

Our Lions: Jayden

Lions

Jayden is the leader of our lion pride. He enjoys lounging in the sun or patrolling his territory. Known for his strength and majestic mane, he's one of the most popular animals in the zoo.

Our Elephants: Orion and Sophie

Elephants

Orion and Sophie are the gentle giants of our zoo. They are often seen splashing in their water pool or enjoying their meals. Their large size and playful nature make them a favorite among visitors.
